Performances

Shobha Choudhary has been performing various types of concerts for more than 20 years. Shobha has performed at many well-known music festivals like Tansen Samaroh, Sawai Gandharv, Karnataka and at many prestigious organizations like National Center for Performing Arts (NCPA), Mumbai.
